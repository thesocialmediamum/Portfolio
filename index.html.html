About Me

Built from scratch. Driven by results.

Hi, I'm Oghenemine. a Content Strategist and Social Media Manager who helps brands fix broken marketing and messaging through content that actually drives revenue.

I've spent the last 3+ years building digital brands from the ground up. including my own. I grew TheSocialMediaMum from zero to 50,000+ followers entirely organically, trained 700+ business owners and delivered real, measurable results for brands across Nigeria, the UK and the USA.

I'm largely self-taught. everything I know I sought out, tested and applied in the real world. That means my knowledge isn't just theoretical. It works.

Currently expanding into Product Marketing, brand positioning and AI-powered content strategy. with a focus on helping SaaS and Fintech companies get their messaging right so the right people buy.
