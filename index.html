About Me

Built from scratch. Driven by results.

Hi, I'm Oghenemine — a Content Strategist and Social Media Manager with 3+ years of experience building brands, growing organic audiences and converting content into revenue.

I've spent the last 3+ years building digital brands from the ground up — including my own. I grew TheSocialMediaMum from zero to 50,000+ followers entirely organically, trained 700+ entrepreneurs and delivered measurable results for clients across Nigeria, the USA and the UK.

I'm largely self-taught — everything I know I sought out, tested and applied in the real world. That means my knowledge isn't just theoretical. It works and it shows in the numbers.

I'm now focused on helping SaaS and Fintech companies get their content strategy, brand messaging and community-led growth right — bringing the same thinking that built a 50K audience to tech brands that want to scale.
